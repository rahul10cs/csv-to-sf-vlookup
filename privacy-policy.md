1. Data Collection This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers managed by the developer. All data processing happens locally within your browser.
2. Salesforce Session Cookies This extension reads your browser's cookies solely to identify your active Salesforce session ID. This session ID is used strictly to perform the data enrichment queries you request (SOQL) directly against your Salesforce instance. The session ID is never shared, logged, or sent to any third party.
3. User Files The CSV files you upload are processed entirely in your browser's memory. They are not uploaded to any server.
